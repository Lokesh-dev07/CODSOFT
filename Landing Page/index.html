Dattakala Shikshan Sanstha

Established in 2009, situated on a picturesque hill top with panoramic views of the Sahyadri Ranges. Providing education from K.G. to P.G. in Pharmacy, Engineering, Management, School and Junior College.

Our Vision & Mission

Shaping Future Leaders

Our vision is to provide pre-eminent educational experiences that are stimulating, responsive to the needs of the present century and prepare students for leadership in their profession and society.

We aim to develop a three-dimensional personality in our students: 1) Pursuit of Knowledge, 2) Commitment to Professionalism, and 3) Inculcation of Values.

Located in a pollution-free environment with fresh air and spacious play fields, our campus provides an enchanting atmosphere conducive to overall student development.
